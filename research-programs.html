Research Programs

Our research covers the full spectrum of topics related to AI and Processes, and to deriving knowledge from process related data: theory, algorithms, applications and software infrastructure. Long-term objectives of understanding AI-enabled Processes are bold and ambitious, and we know that making significant progress in this field can't be done in isolation. That's why our Research Programs are led by research labs in top universities such as Macquarie University (Sydney, Australia), UNSW Sydney (Sydney, Australia), and University of Wollongong (Wollongong, Australia).

The core of the research for the AIP Research Centre is devided into 4 main categories:

  • Industry 4.0:
    • Process Automation
    • IoT-enabled Business Processes
    • Smart Entities and Sensory System
    • Machine-to-Machine and Human-to-Machine Communication
    • Advanced Manufacturing Processes
  • Process Data Science:
    • Process Mining
    • Data Curation for Data-Driven Processes
    • Storytelling with Process Data
    • Process Data Analytics
    • Health Care Analytics
  • Cognitive Technology:
    • Cognitive Assistants for Knowledge Intensive Processes
    • Cognitive Business Process Management
  • Cognitive Science:
    • Cognitive Analytics
    • Understanding Customer's Personality, Behaviour and Attitude
    • Linking Cognitive Science and Process Data Science
