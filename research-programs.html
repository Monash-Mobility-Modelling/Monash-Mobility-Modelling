Research Programs

Our research covers the full spectrum of topics related to Mobility Modelling,which include monitoring, modelling, performance evaluation and design of complex networks (e.g. data and transportation networks), stochastic optimization and control with applications to connected autonomous vehicles (CAVs), intelligent transport systems (ITS) and smart cities.

The core of the research for the Monash Mobility Modelling Group is devided into 4 main categories:

  • Industry 4.0:
    • Process Automation
    • IoT-enabled Business Processes
    • Smart Entities and Sensory System
    • Machine-to-Machine and Human-to-Machine Communication
    • Advanced Manufacturing Processes
  • Process Data Science:
    • Process Mining
    • Data Curation for Data-Driven Processes
    • Storytelling with Process Data
    • Process Data Analytics
    • Health Care Analytics
  • Cognitive Technology:
    • Cognitive Assistants for Knowledge Intensive Processes
    • Cognitive Business Process Management
  • Cognitive Science:
    • Cognitive Analytics
    • Understanding Customer's Personality, Behaviour and Attitude
    • Linking Cognitive Science and Process Data Science
